CARIT:

This is the website for the Center for Applied Research Center in Information Technology (CARIT). The CARIT is a research center meant to provide survey research, evaluation of programs, performance measurement, and experimental research. The CARIT may be used by staff who need assistance with projects, grants, or contracts. It will serve as an environment which provides Kennesaw State University students with research experience.
